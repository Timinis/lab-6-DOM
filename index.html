About Us

We are a locally owned company started by our founder Salmon McSalmon. He was working an unfulfilling live and a magical salmon granted him the vision to start a salmon cofee store. with this new goal he quit his job and soon became a hit in Seattle. Now he is living a wonderful life with his wife and 2 kids.
